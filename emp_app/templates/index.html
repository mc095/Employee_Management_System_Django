Features of the Employee Management System

  • ✔️ View All Employees: Access a complete list of all employees in the system.
  • ✔️ Add New Employee: Easily add a new employee with their details.
  • ✔️ Remove Employee: Remove any employee from the system by their ID.
  • ✔️ Filter Employee Details: Search and filter employees based on their names or roles.
  • ✔️ Responsive Design: Accessible on various devices, thanks to Bootstrap.
  • ✔️ Data Validation: Ensure that all employee data is entered correctly.
